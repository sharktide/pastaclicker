Pasta Clicker

How to play

Each upgrade has 5 levels and each level costs 1.5 times the subsequent level.

Types of Upgrades
  • Red-orange - Pasta Per Click Upgrades
  • Blue - Auto pasta per second
  • Green - Multipliers per click
    • List of upgrades
  • Red-orange - Paper Plate: +1 pasta per click per level
  • Red-orange- Plastic Plate: +5 pasta per click per level
  • Red-orange - Glass Plate: +10 pasta per click per level
  • Red-orange - Bronze Plate: +15 pasta per click per level
  • Blue - Cook Helper: +1 pasta per second per level
  • Blue - Chef Assistant: +5 pasta per second per level
  • Blue - Master Chef: +10 pasta per second per level
  • Green - Fusili: +25% multiplier, +5% for subsequent levels
  • Green - Ravioli: +50% multiplier, +10% for subsequent levels
  • Green - Penne: +75% multiplier, +15% for subsequent levels
